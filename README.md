## Key Themes & Preparation Strategy

> **Experian's Innovation Engineering team values people who can bridge research and production.** Preparation should focus on demonstrating that you are not just academically capable — you ship results, collaborate openly, and adapt fast.

### 🎯 Core Themes to Emphasize

- **Applied ML Impact** — Show that your models produce measurable outcomes, not just experiments.
- **Generative AI Curiosity** — Demonstrate hands-on familiarity with transformer-based and diffusion models.
- **Engineering Discipline** — Highlight clean code, version control, reproducibility, and documentation habits.
- **Collaboration** — Prove you work well in cross-functional teams with non-technical stakeholders.
- **Learning Velocity** — Show examples of rapidly picking up unfamiliar tools and contributing quickly.

### 🗓 Recommended Preparation Schedule

| Day | Focus |
|-----|-------|
| Day 1 | Read job posting; complete role-fit analysis |
| Day 2 | Write and rehearse STAR story bank |
| Day 3 | Practice mock interview Q&As (timed, recorded) |
| Day 4 | Drill Sova answers to 90 seconds each |
| Day 5 | Full mock Sova session; review and refine |
